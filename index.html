What Can We Do For You?

Ensuring your success on AWS is our main priority. Whether you are a one-man start-up or global enterprise, we will guide you through the world of almost limitless AWS opportunities to reap the greatest value possible!

Pre-sale activities

Having successfully completed numerous projects, our team possesses both technical solutions and expertise to implement pre-sale activities the right way. Our approach ensures that the pre-sale phase is well aligned with the customer’s requirements and corresponds to all the highly venerated pre-sale practices.

Refactoring

The advent of cloud computing brought about the amount of advantages too numerous to overlook. AWS is the leading cloud computing service offering a scalable, low-cost, and highly reliable infrastructure platform. In addition, the prospect of utilizing secure connections to data and high-performance capabilities makes AWS cloud a good choice to drive your enterprise forward.

AWS based Architecture desing

Every software product requires a thoroughly structured and advanced software architecture. However, this is far from essential in the modern day reality. Being the groundwork of any project, it is vital for your software architecture to be flexible enough to conform into the broad AWS ecosystem. Be it existing or non-existing software, we will seamlessly tailor its architecture to AWS’s needs.

AWS Trainings

We offer conclusive programs based on case studies and discussions of various issues. Our team of professionals constantly strives to educate you to make informed decisions about IT solutions based on business requirements. Big data and infrastructure services, cloud adoption, security and compliance, etc. are all made clear with us!

Chatbots

It is difficult to overestimate the contribution of chatbots into scaling business teams in their relations with customers. We are eager to set up a chatbot functionality with customization and superior customer experience for you. Chatbots do a great job at simplifying complex processes to streamline and automate common and repetitive tasks. And our team does know how to get the most out of them!

AWS Consulting

It is simple to get lost in the intricacies of the immense Amazon Web Services universe. Our team possesses the capabilities necessary to design, architect, build, migrate, and manage your workloads and applications on AWS. Whether you are building a new or desire to migrate an existing application, the AWS Galaxy is open to pitch in our skill and competence you in deploying and managing cloud architectures of any complexity.
